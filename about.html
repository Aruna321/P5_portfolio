Life in Brief

Working through highschool at increasingly dubious locales I graduated after lettering in cross country and track twice as well as band all four years.
I then went to Missouri to attend Wentworth Military Academy for a year as a preparatory school for the Airforce Military Academy. There I learned exactly what I didn't want to do with my life and left to attend WSU as an engineering major finding again something I didn't want to do.
Two years at SFCC gave me a strong computer science background which I ended up using as my minor in pursuit of Visual Design Communications at EWU.

Interests

Having a penchant for unusual activities I snowboard, mt. bike, parkour, ice skate, and perhaps most exciting of all I rock climb!
Musically I have an extensive piano background as well as many years of trombone and guitar and some vocal experience as well.
